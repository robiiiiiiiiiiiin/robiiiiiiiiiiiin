**Maya: A nation of brilliant astronomers**

Around 600 AD, the Maya people inexplicably left their tribal territory, which they had dotted over centuries with enormous and magnificent religious buildings, pyramids, roads, and other structures. Who or what could have prompted this cultured people to undertake such a mass migration?

The most complex calendar system in the world served solely to calculate their return. But why did they start their calendar on August 11, 3114 BC? What will happen when it ends on December 23, 2012, the magical year when, according to the Maya, “a new era begins”?
