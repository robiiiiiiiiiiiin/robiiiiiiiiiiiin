In the "Contact" brochure, there is a paragraph about the Incan Golden Airplanes.

Some golden miniature "airplanes" have been found in Colombia. Some say that these are miniature representations of ancient aircraft while some says these are just representations of birds and fish.

Arthur Poyslee was the first to claim, in the 70's, that these artifacts were actually able to fly. 20 years later, the three other guys built exact replicas increased in size by a factor of 16 and equipped them with engines and radio control systems. In 1997 they performed a flight test at the Ancient Astronauts Society World Conference and the replicas were actually able to lift off, fly and perform maneuvers. The obvious conclusion was that the Inca objects were proven to be actual replicas of 100% functional aircrafts.
